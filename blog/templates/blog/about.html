Swiss governance and family office operations

Building a calmer operating office for cross-border wealth.

Hashen supports entrepreneurs, family offices, and German-speaking HNWI families who need Swiss operating clarity across governance, reporting, accounting, payroll, and Bitcoin-aware treasury thinking.

Many internationally mobile entrepreneurs and families do not only need investment ideas. They need a disciplined operating layer: clean accounts, reliable payroll, clear month-end reporting, well-documented governance, and coordination between banks, tax advisers, lawyers, trustees, asset managers, and company organs.

Hashen is being built for that practical layer. The focus is on Swiss coordination, reporting, accounting and payroll operations, governance support, and treasury research for families and companies that operate across traditional wealth and digital capital.

The positioning is deliberately compliance-ready. Each mandate should be classified before work starts: pure operating support, governance coordination, accounting or payroll work, corporate administration, relocation coordination, or work that requires AML/KYC handling or a regulated partner.

Hashen does not position itself as an exchange, broker, trading desk, asset manager, trustee, or casual fiduciary concierge. Where asset management, banking, trustee, tax, legal, or regulated financial services are required, the model is to work with appropriately licensed or qualified partners.

For family offices, this creates a useful middle layer: practical enough to handle accounting, payroll, reporting, document discipline, and coordination; careful enough to separate advisory, governance, and regulated activities; and modern enough to understand Bitcoin as a long-term monetary asset rather than a short-term trade.

Bitcoin remains central to the research view. It is open, scarce, global, and independent settlement infrastructure. Around it, we see opportunities in treasury policy, ownership reporting, cross-border payments, and capital structures that are better suited to a digital age.

The goal is simple: give families and entrepreneurs a calmer Swiss operating base from which capital, companies, and responsibilities can be understood clearly.

We exist to provide the tools, reporting discipline, research, and partner coordination needed to act with independence, restraint, and a long time horizon.
