At Sick Ventures, accessible from www.sick-ventures.com, one of the priorities is the privacy of visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information that are collected and recorded by Sick Ventures and how they are used

Which personal data do we collect, and when?

We collect information that you voluntarily send to us when you engage with us through various means such as email, contact forms, or other communication channels. This typically includes personal data such as your name, email address, telephone number, payment details, language preferences, and possibly your job title and business address.

For what purposes do we process your personal data?

We process your personal data in connection with our activities, which may include service provision, member management, or as part of a commercial transaction. Specifically, we process your personal data to send you event-related information, newsletters, or other updates, and to transact with you when you purchase products or services from our website. >The personal information that you are asked to provide, and the reasons why you are asked to provide it, will be made clear to you at the point we ask you to provide your personal information. If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide. In summary, the information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Improve and expand our offerings
  • Understand and analyze user preferences and website usage patterns
  • Develop new products, services, and features
  • Communicate with you for customer service, updates, and marketing
  • Send you emails and newsletters that you have opted in to receive
  • Prevent and detect fraud or other prohibited activities
  • Comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements

What is our justification for processing your personal data and do we share it with third parties?

We process your personal data based on your consent, especially when you subscribe to receive newsletters or other communications. If required, we may also process data to fulfill contracts or, where permissible, to serve our legitimate interests, such as for marketing, legal claims, or if it is necessary for the administration and operation of our business.

We do not disclose your personal data to third parties except as necessary for our service providers who assist us in our operations, particularly regarding IT and system administration.

Where are your personal data stored and for how long?

Sick Ventures is based in Switzerland, and processes and store all personal data within Switzerland on servers that are subject to the countires data protection laws.

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, to comply with legal obligations, or as long as it is required for the duration of any contractual relationship. Once no longer needed, we take steps to delete or anonymize your data.

How do we protect your personal data and what are your rights?

We employ appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. We also work closely with our hosting providers to secure the databases against breaches. However, while we strive for the highest level of security, we must note that no system is infallible.

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your data, as provided under applicable data protection laws. If you wish to exercise these rights, you may be required to provide proof of identity. Additionally, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Cookies and Server Log files

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help websites remember information about your visit. Sick Ventures uses cookies to understand how you use our website and to enhance your user experience, including tailoring content and advertisements. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your computer. If you choose to refuse our cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service.

Our hosting provider collects data in server log files to maintain website security and performance. This data is processed in accordance with legal requirements and is not used for any other purposes.

Google Analytics and Newsletter

We use Google Analytics to optimize our website and services. You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking with browser add-ons or settings.

We offer newsletters and may use a provider that allows us to manage subscriptions and analyze performance. You can unsubscribe at any time, and we respect all opt-out requests.
